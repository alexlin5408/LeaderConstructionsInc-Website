Who We Are

Leaders Construction Inc. was founded in 2020. Our team members have over 20 years of professional experience in the area of project management, design, permitting, and construction. Our foundation is built on:

  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Hard Work

We are experienced on budgeting, scheduling, designing and completing construction projects successfully. We are familiar with governmental codes, rules, and regulations.

Our Goal

Our goal is to meet our client's needs and complete the design and construction that fit the client's budget and schedule while meeting local governmental codes, rules, and regulations.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in the construction industry, known for excellence, innovation, and unwavering integrity.
